Build a pendulum wave demo: 24 pendulums side by side with lengths tuned so their frequencies drift in and out of phase, producing traveling waves, ghosting, and chaos-then-order patterns on a 60-second cycle. Render bobs with trails, and beneath them draw a persistence chart tracing each bob x-position over time like a seismograph. Flash a subtle marker at pattern resets. No interaction.
